Recommended Books

“All The Bright Places”

by Jennifer Niven

All the Bright Places takes place in modern day Indiana and follows two teenagers named Violet and Finch. Finch is a boy with a harsh past who struggles with mental illness, even though his family and friends won't admit it.

User Review

"Enchanting and beautifully written. A mesmerizing read from start to finish!"

“Regretting You”

by Colleen Hoover

Morgan Grant and her sixteen-year-old daughter, Clara, would like nothing more than to be nothing alike. Morgan is determined to prevent her daughter from making the same mistakes she did. By getting pregnant and married way too young, Morgan put her own dreams on hold.

User Review

"Powerful and inspiring. This story of resilience stayed with me long after reading."

“Where the Crawdads Sing”

by Delia Owens

A coming-of-age story and murder mystery set in the marshes of North Carolina, weaving nature and human emotion.

User Review

"Absolutely gripping! The descriptive writing makes you feel part of the story."

“Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind”

by Yuval Noah Harari

A fascinating overview of the history of humans from the Stone Age to the modern age, exploring how biology and history have defined us.

User Review

"Eye-opening and thought-provoking. A must-read for history lovers."

“The Alchemist”

by Paulo Coelho

A mystical tale of Santiago, a shepherd boy who dreams of finding treasure and discovering his personal legend.

User Review

"Inspirational and poetic. Encourages you to follow your dreams."

“Becoming”

by Michelle Obama

The former First Lady shares her deeply personal journey from childhood to life in the White House.

User Review

"Honest and uplifting. A beautifully told story of strength and perseverance."

“1984”

by George Orwell

A dystopian novel about a totalitarian regime that controls every aspect of life and the consequences of mass surveillance.

User Review

"Chilling and profound. A classic that remains relevant today."

“Little Fires Everywhere”

by Celeste Ng

A story exploring the intertwined lives of the Richardson family and an enigmatic mother and daughter who upend their lives.

User Review

"Compelling and emotional. Explores motherhood and secrets with nuance."

“The Silent Patient”

by Alex Michaelides

A psychological thriller about a woman who stops speaking after committing a shocking act and the therapist determined to uncover why.

User Review

"Page-turner with an unexpected twist. Couldn't put it down!"

“Circe”

by Madeline Miller

A retelling of the story of Circe, a powerful witch from Greek mythology, as she finds her own path and identity.

User Review

"Beautifully written and mythically enchanting. A fresh take on an ancient tale."

“The Book Thief”

by Markus Zusak

Set in Nazi Germany, a young girl finds solace in stealing books and sharing them during dark times.

User Review

"Heartbreaking yet hopeful. A story that stays with you."

“Pachinko”

by Min Jin Lee

An epic family saga about Korean immigrants in Japan, dealing with identity, survival, and hope across generations.

User Review

"Powerful and moving. A beautifully crafted multi-generational story."
